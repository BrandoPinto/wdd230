Arequipa Chamber of Commerce - Site Plan

Site Name

Arequipa Chamber of Commerce

This name was selected as it represents the city of Arequipa, where the chamber operates to support local businesses and economic development.

Site Purpose

The Arequipa Chamber of Commerce website aims to:

  • Act as a regional economic catalyst, providing resources and support to local businesses.
  • Serve as a hub for community events and networking opportunities.

Scenarios

Visitors to the site might ask:

  • What events will the chamber be holding this month that promote business-to-business networking?
  • Where can I find contact information for the chamber's board of directors?
  • What has been the population growth in the area?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site includes:

  • Primary Color: #800000 (Used for headings and the header background)
  • Secondary Color: #FFD700 (Used for accents and call-to-action buttons)

Typography

The fonts for the site include:

  • Headings: 'Montserrat', sans-serif
  • Body Text: 'Open Sans', sans-serif
